My games from GAME 601 - Game Prototyping at American University in Fall 2021

Game Modification: The Number Guessing Game
A modified number guessing game, give game participants have a more balanced chance of winning - win or lose is not decided by whether you hit the correct number in a large range now, but based on your total score after a few rounds (score are gained based on the difference between your answer and the correct number). Click the button to see the original rule, modified rule and design process.

(Midterm) Non-Digital Game: Dive In Abyss
A simple card game, in which players act as different identities, use your Asset Points to hire Partners and ask them to execute your orders for earn more Asset Points or enough Reputation Points. Different player identities, partner cards, action cards and event cards are designed. Click the button for detailed rules and designed cards.

Hypertext Fiction: Sudden Little Trip
Most of people live in a circle, get used to this circle, and repeat this circle - which is called EVERYDAY LIFE. One day, if a sudden guest comes to you and exceeds your imagination... What will you do? Stay in your circle, or try to add something new?
